Paredes Garza Swiss Italian Adventure

June 15 - June 24, 2024

Journey Overview

Join us on an unforgettable journey through Switzerland and Italy, where we'll experience the perfect blend of cultural outings, outdoor activities, and gastronomic experiences. Our adventure begins in the vibrant city of Zurich, takes us through the majestic Swiss Alps in St. Moritz and Zermatt, and then ventures into Italy's most iconic cities - Milan, Bologna, and optionally Rome.

This carefully curated itinerary combines luxury accommodations with authentic local experiences, ensuring memorable moments for everyone. From Swiss chocolate tasting to Italian pasta making, from mountain hikes to museum visits, every day brings new discoveries.

Zurich

Night 1: Sun, Jun 15 - Mon, Jun 16

Zurich Marriott Hotel

  • Morning: Start the day with coffee and pastries at a local café. Consider visiting Confiserie Sprüngli for an authentic Swiss breakfast experience.
  • Late Morning: Head to the Swiss National Museum to explore Swiss cultural history, which should be engaging for both adults and teens.
  • Lunch: Enjoy lunch at Haus Hiltl, the world's oldest vegetarian restaurant, offering a diverse range of dishes.
  • Afternoon: Take a scenic Lake Zurich boat cruise, a relaxing way to admire the city's beauty from the water. Suitable for all ages.
  • Evening: Dine at the Zunfthaus zur Waag, offering traditional Swiss cuisine in a historic guild house setting.
  • Late Evening:Take a stroll through the Old Town (Altstadt)

Links:

  • Confiserie Sprüngli
  • Swiss National Museum tour
  • Lake Zurich boat cruise
  • Grossmünster Cathedral visit
  • Evening stroll through Old Town (Altstadt)

Travel from Zurich to St. Moritz

We will be using the Swiss Travel Pass for this journey. The departure times are flexible, allowing us to enjoy the scenic views at our own pace.

St. Moritz

Night 2: Sun, Jun 16 - Mon, Jun 17

Hotel Grace La Margna St. Moritz

  • Morning: Enjoy a leisurely breakfast at your hotel before exploring St. Moritz Village.
  • Late Morning: Visit the Engadiner Museum for insights into the Engadin Valley's culture and history.
  • Lunch: Try a local trattoria for lunch, with dishes featuring fresh ingredients from the region.
  • Afternoon: Embark on a picturesque lake walk or light hiking in the nearby Swiss Alps, offering breathtaking views and easy trails for families.
  • Evening: Relax with family dining at the renowned Badrutt's Palace or similar for a mix of local and international cuisine.

Links:

  • St. Moritz Village exploration
  • Engadiner Museum visit
  • Swiss Alps hiking
  • Muottas Muragl funicular ride
  • Spa experience at hotel
